25-11-2003 21:17:57

I saw them in Cardiff.

They played most of their cool stuff, with the notable exceptions of 'Creep' and 'Street Spirit'.

They played a lot of their new stuff (which I quite like). Idioteque and 2+2=5 stick in my head as two excellent examples of their new stuff. They also played their older stuff, like Paranoid Android, Just and Iron Lung. They didn't play "No Surprises" either, which would have been cool, especially due to it's political undertones.

They didn't do any covers, it's not their thing. Asian Dub Foundation (who supported them) were excellent and I think they did some covers, although I didn't recognise any of their own stuff to know if it was a cover or not.

His eye didn't look at me directly, although he was giving the crowd a lot of it.

I simply can't get over how amazing they were. They blow away any other band I've seen live. They were just that good.

--Matt
